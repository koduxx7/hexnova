Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our web applications

What makes HexNova's apps different?

Our apps are built with a focus on user experience, performance, and innovation. We combine cutting-edge technology with thoughtful design to create applications that are both powerful and easy to use.

How do I get started with Clarity?

Getting started with Clarity is simple! Just sign up for an account, and you'll have immediate access to all features. Our intuitive onboarding process will guide you through the basics of note-taking and task management.

Are your apps free to use?

We offer both free and premium tiers for our applications. The free tier includes essential features, while our premium tier provides advanced capabilities and additional storage. You can upgrade anytime to unlock more features.

How often do you release new features?

We're constantly working on improving our applications. We release updates regularly based on user feedback and technological advancements. Our development team ensures that new features are thoroughly tested before release.

Can I use your apps offline?

Yes! All our applications are built as Progressive Web Apps (PWAs), which means you can use them offline. Your data will sync automatically when you're back online.

How do you protect my data?

We take data security seriously. All our applications use end-to-end encryption, and we follow industry best practices for data protection. Your information is stored securely and is never shared with third parties.
